What is Cards Against Development?

Cards Against Development is a wickedly funny party game designed for developers, tech enthusiasts, and anyone with a twisted sense of humor. It's a unique spin-off of the popular game Cards Against Humanity, tailored specifically to the world of software development.

How to Play

Playing Cards Against Development is simple. Each round, one player becomes the "Card Czar" and reads a question or a fill-in-the-blank statement from a black card. The other players choose the funniest white card from their hand to complete the sentence, and the Card Czar picks the winner. The goal? To create the most outrageous, hilarious, and politically incorrect combinations! Complete Rules

Features

  • Unleash Your Developer Humor: Enjoy a game that speaks directly to developers, filled with inside jokes, programming puns, and tech references.
  • Endless Fun: With a vast collection of black and white cards, every game is a unique and hilarious experience that will have you and your friends laughing for hours.
  • Expandable Content: Keep the game fresh and exciting with expansion packs containing even more outrageous cards to add to your collection.
  • Play Anywhere: Whether you're at a party, game night, or coding hackathon, Cards Against Development is the perfect icebreaker to get the laughter flowing.
  • Accessible to All: You don't need to be a coding expert to enjoy this game. It's designed to entertain both developers and those with a general interest in technology.
